Welcome to Y N Hosakote

Y. N. Hosakote (Yallappa Nayakana Hosakote) is a historic town in Pavagada Taluk of Tumakuru District, Karnataka. The town was established around a hill fort built by the local ruler Yallappa Nayaka in the 16th century and continues to be an important local centre today.

Contact on WhatsApp

About the Town

Y N Hosakote serves as a regional hub for nearby villages and has its own panchayat, police station and post office. The official language spoken here is Kannada, and the town connects surrounding rural communities with services and markets.

Local Economy & Culture

Silk Saree Weaving

The town is well known for traditional handloom silk saree weaving and textile design activities.

Agriculture

Groundnut cultivation and dry land farming are important occupations in the surrounding rural areas.

Community Life

Festivals, temples and local markets form the centre of social and cultural life of residents.
