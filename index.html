Welcome to Y N Hosakote

Y. N. Hosakote (Yallappa Nayakana Hosakote) is a historic town in Pavagada Taluk of Tumakuru District, Karnataka. The town was established around a hill fort built by the local ruler Yallappa Nayaka in the 16th century and remains an important local centre.

About the Town

Y N Hosakote serves as a regional hub for nearby villages and provides administrative, market and service connections for surrounding rural communities.

Geography & Location

Y N Hosakote is located in Pavagada Taluk of Tumakuru District in Karnataka. The town lies in a semi-arid region of the Deccan Plateau and experiences hot summers and moderate winters. It serves as a service and trading centre for surrounding villages and agricultural communities.

Administration & Public Services

Gram Panchayat

The village panchayat manages local administration, sanitation, water supply and welfare programs.

Post Office

The local post office provides postal, savings and government services for residents and nearby villages.

Police Services

Law and order services support public safety and assistance for surrounding rural areas.

Education

Government and private schools in the town serve students from nearby villages. For higher education, students travel to Pavagada, Madhugiri and Tumakuru.

Transport & Connectivity

Road Connectivity

The town is connected by regional roads to Pavagada and neighbouring towns.

Bus Services

KSRTC and private buses operate regular services connecting nearby villages and towns.

Nearby Towns

Pavagada, Madhugiri and Hindupur act as major commercial and transport centres.

Local Economy & Culture

Textile Craft

Traditional weaving and small textile related occupations support many families.

Agriculture

Groundnut farming and dry land agriculture dominate the surrounding countryside.

Community Life

Temples, fairs and weekly markets form the social and cultural identity of the town.
